How Studying on Listudy Works

Listudy uses the Leitner system for managing "cards". Each move in a study represents a card. When you make the right move in a possition the card is moved up in the boxes. When you make a move that is not in the study the card is put back in the first box. Cards from lower boxes are choosen first when picking a card to repeat.
